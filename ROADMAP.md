# Roadmap

> **Note:** This roadmap describes potential future directions for JS Tap-Tap.
> Nothing here is promised or scheduled. Items may be added, changed, or dropped
> at any time as priorities evolve.

---

## Near-Term (v1.1)

- [ ] **Difficulty modes** — Easy (slower spawns), Normal (current), Hard (faster spawns, shorter lifetime).
- [ ] **Configurable game duration** — Let players choose 15s, 30s, or 60s rounds.
- [ ] **Sound effects** — Click sound on target hit, countdown beep in last 5 seconds.
- [ ] **Visual feedback on hit** — Brief flash/particle effect when a target is clicked.
- [ ] **Password reset** — Allow users to reset their password (e.g. by deleting their account and re-registering from within the app, rather than editing JSON manually).

---

## Mid-Term (v1.x)

- [ ] **Local leaderboard screen** — View the top scores across all registered users on the same machine.
- [ ] **Game statistics** — Track targets missed, click accuracy percentage, and average score over sessions.
- [ ] **Animated title screen** — Replace the static login screen with a brief animated intro.
- [ ] **Resolution scaling** — Support window sizes beyond 800×600 for larger monitors.
- [ ] **Linux/macOS support** — Expand platform support; provide platform-specific installer scripts.

---

## Long-Term / Exploratory

- [ ] **Online highscore board** — Optional, opt-in cloud submission of highscores (requires significant privacy/security engineering — out of scope for a local game without a backend).
- [ ] **Multiplayer (local, same machine, split keyboard)** — Two players competing simultaneously.
- [ ] **Custom themes / skins** — Colour theme selection from a settings screen.
- [ ] **Accessibility options** — High-contrast mode, larger targets, extended time option.
